About InAmigos

The InAmigos foundation is a chattisgarh-based section 8 non-profit organization founded by Govind shukla in september 2020. Driven by a commitment to social wefare,it operates PAN-India to address critical societal issues, ranging from poverty alleviation and child education to women's empowerment and animal wefare.

Our Ongoing Projects
